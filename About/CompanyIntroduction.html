Company Introduction

XingYuan machinery is a global leader in the design and manufacturing of die casting tooling, magnesium and aluminum die casting, CNC machining, secondary finishing and assembly.

We serve all markets across Asia, Europe and North America. After twenty-four years of development, XingYuan has been recognized as a High Technology Enterprise.

Our attention to Quality has allowed us to provide superior products across the globe to the most exacting of standards. Over the twenty-four years since its inception, XingYuan Machinery has always been dedicated to its Quality First principle, and to serving its customers with the highest level of excellence.

天津北宿科技有限公司是致力于永磁分选行业的专业设备制造商，依托德国领先的磁力分选和金属探测技术， 以设计制造高性能设备为目标，为业界提供高性能高品质设计的磁力分选和金属探测设备。 凭借丰富的专业技术领域的应用经验，为企业解决产品加工、纯化以及质量评审中遇到的难题。

产品应用涵盖：食品、医药、化工、电池、烟草、塑料、玻璃、陶瓷、造纸、颜料、水泥、金属加工等诸多领域。
