QUALITY MANAGEMENT

  • Laboratory
  • Process Control

Quality is our first and foremost priority. To support this philosophy, we have invested in the finest equipment to manage our processes, raw material and components that require additional screening. We analyze and monitor all incoming raw material for chemistry specifications and maintain full lot traceability to assure the properties of the castings in your assemblies. Our two Zeiss CMM's, 3-D laser scanning systems and full complement of bench gages allows us to measure every physical dimension conceivable. Our X-Ray systems allows us to sample critical components used in life safety and mission critical parts to assure structural integrity of the production.

天津北宿科技有限公司是致力于永磁分选行业的专业设备制造商，依托德国领先的磁力分选和金属探测技术， 以设计制造高性能设备为目标，为业界提供高性能高品质设计的磁力分选和金属探测设备。 凭借丰富的专业技术领域的应用经验，为企业解决产品加工、纯化以及质量评审中遇到的难题。
